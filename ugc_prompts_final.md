## SHOT 1 -- Gift Wrapping (0-5 s)

### Full Prompt

Handheld iPhone 14 vertical video, 9:16 aspect ratio, filmed from above POV looking down at a messy kitchen table. A young woman's hands (age 25-30) are carefully wrapping a small Japanese glass wind chime (furin) in crinkled cream washi paper. Her nails have chipped dark-red nail polish, cuticles slightly ragged, thin silver ring on right index finger. The kitchen table is cluttered: a half-empty coffee mug with a brown ring stain on the wood surface, a small pile of unsorted mail and envelopes, a roll of tape partially unrolled, a pen with the cap off, breadcrumbs near the edge. Daylight from a window to the left creates harsh asymmetric lighting with a strong shadow falling across the right side of the table. Slight overexposure on the left edge of frame where the window light hits directly. The video has subtle hand tremor from the person holding the phone with one hand while wrapping with the other. Shallow depth of field, slight motion blur on the moving hand. NOT cinematic, NOT color graded, NOT studio lighting, NOT perfect composition. Raw, authentic, amateur home video look with slight warmth from auto white balance.

### Environment Checklist (Expert 5)

- [ ] Cluttered kitchen table surface (wood or laminate, not pristine)
- [ ] Coffee mug with visible ring stain on table
- [ ] Scattered unsorted mail / envelopes (at least 3 pieces)
- [ ] Tape roll, partially unrolled
- [ ] Pen with cap off
- [ ] Breadcrumbs or food residue near table edge
- [ ] Cream/off-white washi paper, visibly crinkled (not fresh from package)
- [ ] Asymmetric daylight from one side, harsh shadow on opposite side
- [ ] Overexposed patch where window light hits

### UGC Test Criteria

A viewer must perceive this as a real person filming themselves wrapping a gift at home. The messy table, imperfect nails, and shaky POV angle must feel like a casual phone recording, not a product demo. If a viewer's first instinct is "someone sent this on WhatsApp," the shot passes.

### Generation Notes

- **Model**: Kling 2.1 (best for close-up hand detail and surface textures)
- **Variations**: Generate 8-10. Select for best hand realism (nail imperfections visible) and most natural table clutter arrangement.
- **Watch for**: AI tends to make nails too perfect and tables too clean. Reject any output with glossy/manicured nails or symmetrically arranged objects.

## SHOT 2 -- Mother Unwraps Gift (5-10 s)

### Full Prompt

Handheld iPhone 13 vertical video, 9:16 aspect ratio, filmed from slightly above and in front (daughter's POV standing across the table). A mature woman's hands (age 55-60) are unwrapping a small package wrapped in crinkled washi paper. Her hands show prominent veins across the backs, two or three brown age spots near the knuckles, dry skin around the thumb, short unpainted nails with slight ridges. She wears a worn gold wedding band on her left ring finger, slightly loose. The table has a floral tablecloth that is bunched and uneven on one side. Next to her hands: a half-drunk cup of tea in a mismatched ceramic mug, a pair of reading glasses with one arm folded, a crumpled paper napkin. The lighting is warm interior overhead kitchen light, slightly yellow, creating unflattering shadows under the fingers. A small bright spot from an overhead fixture reflects on the table surface. The camera wobbles slightly as the person filming adjusts their stance. Slight digital noise in the shadow areas. NOT cinematic, NOT studio, NOT perfect skin, NOT commercial lighting. This looks like a daughter recording her mother opening a present at the kitchen table on a weekday evening.

### Environment Checklist (Expert 5)

- [ ] Floral tablecloth, visibly bunched/uneven (not ironed flat)
- [ ] Half-drunk tea in a ceramic mug (not matching the tablecloth pattern)
- [ ] Reading glasses, one arm folded or placed haphazardly
- [ ] Crumpled paper napkin
- [ ] Warm overhead light, slightly yellow cast
- [ ] Unflattering shadow under the hands from overhead source
- [ ] Bright spot / light reflection on table surface
- [ ] Crinkled washi paper being opened (not pristine wrapping)

### UGC Test Criteria

Hands must read as belonging to a real 55+ woman: veins, age spots, and dry skin are non-negotiable. The wedding band must look worn (not shiny-new). The tablecloth must look lived-in. If a viewer thinks "that looks like my mom's kitchen," the shot passes.

### Generation Notes

- **Model**: Kling 2.1 (superior hand/skin texture rendering)
- **Variations**: Generate 12-15. The critical selection criterion is hand aging realism. Reject any output where hands look younger than 50 or skin is too smooth.
- **Watch for**: AI loves to de-age hands. Insist on veins, spots, and skin texture. Also watch for wedding bands that look like 3D renders (too reflective, too perfect).

## SHOT 3 -- Daughter Records Video Message (10-15 s)

### Full Prompt

iPhone 14 front-facing camera selfie video, vertical 9:16, held at arm's length by a young woman (age 25) sitting in a garden chair. She is recording a personal video message, looking directly into the camera with a nervous but genuine smile. Her hair is messy, partly pulled back with loose strands across her forehead, slight frizz from humidity. No makeup visible, a small blemish on her chin, slightly uneven skin tone, natural eyebrows. She wears a faded oversized t-shirt. Behind her: a sunny but unkempt garden with overgrown bushes, a weathered white plastic garden chair with dirt marks, a green watering can on its side on the grass, patchy lawn with some brown spots. The sunlight is harsh midday from above, creating strong shadows under her eyes and nose. The front camera produces slightly softer image quality with visible digital grain. Her arm holding the phone creates a slight wobble. She starts speaking, her expression shifts between nervous laughter and sincerity, lips slightly trembling. NOT studio, NOT ring light, NOT influencer, NOT filtered, NOT color corrected. This is a real young woman filming an emotional video in her parents' backyard.

### Environment Checklist (Expert 5)

- [ ] Overgrown garden bushes (not trimmed, slightly wild)
- [ ] Weathered white plastic chair with visible dirt marks or discoloration
- [ ] Watering can on its side on the grass (not placed upright neatly)
- [ ] Patchy lawn with brown spots
- [ ] Harsh overhead sunlight, unflattering shadows on face
- [ ] Faded/worn t-shirt (not crisp or new-looking)
- [ ] No garden furniture arrangement -- objects placed randomly

### UGC Test Criteria

The woman must look like a real person having an unplanned emotional moment, not an actress or influencer. Key tells: no ring light reflection in eyes, no makeup, visible skin imperfections, messy hair. The garden must look like an actual suburban backyard that hasn't been landscaped recently. If a viewer thinks "she's about to cry recording this for her mom," the shot passes.

### Generation Notes

- **Model**: Kling 2.1 or Sora (test both -- front-camera selfie style is a strength of Sora)
- **Variations**: Generate 10-12. Select for most natural facial expression and most convincing "nervous sincerity." Reject any output where she looks posed, confident, or where skin is too smooth.
- **Watch for**: AI tends to add ring light reflections and smooth skin. Also tends to make gardens look too manicured. Reject any output that looks like an Instagram story.

## SHOT 4 -- Mother Watches Video, Emotion Transition (15-20 s) -- CRITICAL SHOT

### Full Prompt

Handheld iPhone 13 vertical video, 9:16, filmed from the side at roughly 45 degrees by a third person standing a few feet away. A woman (age 55-60) is sitting on a couch holding a smartphone in both hands, watching a video message on the phone screen. Her face transitions from neutral curiosity (slight squint, head tilted) to recognition to rising emotion to tears over the course of 5 seconds. Her face: visible wrinkles around eyes and mouth, no makeup at all, gray roots showing at the hairline through dark brown dyed hair, a small dark mole on her left cheek near the jawline, slightly puffy under-eyes, dry lips. She wears a faded beige cardigan with a small pull in the knit near the shoulder, over a plain dark top. Behind her on the wall: 3-4 framed family photos of various sizes in mismatched frames, slightly crooked, one frame has visible dust on top. A floor lamp with a yellowish shade casts warm uneven light from the left side. The right side of her face falls into softer shadow. The camera operator breathes audibly (ambient sound). The phone screen glows on her face from below, creating slight under-lighting. Her hands grip the phone tightly, knuckles whitening, veins visible. A single tear forms and rolls down her right cheek. Her chin trembles. NOT cinematic, NOT color graded, NOT acted, NOT symmetrical composition. This is someone secretly filming their mother having a genuine emotional breakdown watching her daughter's video message.

### Environment Checklist (Expert 5)

- [ ] Couch/sofa (not pristine -- visible wear, maybe a throw blanket bunched to one side)
- [ ] 3-4 framed family photos on wall, mismatched frames, slightly crooked
- [ ] Dust visible on top of at least one frame
- [ ] Floor lamp with yellowish/warm shade, casting uneven light
- [ ] Faded beige cardigan with a visible pull/snag in the knit
- [ ] Phone screen glow illuminating her face from below
- [ ] Warm, cluttered living room feel -- not minimalist, not staged

### UGC Test Criteria

This is the money shot. The emotion transition must feel INVOLUNTARY -- not performed. Key indicators: the tear must form naturally (not already crying from frame one), chin tremble must be subtle, the shift from curiosity to tears must feel like it catches her off guard. The face must have every imperfection listed (wrinkles, mole, gray roots, no makeup). If a viewer feels their own throat tighten watching this, the shot passes. If it looks like an actress crying, it fails.

### Generation Notes

- **Model**: Kling 2.1 (best for subtle facial micro-expression transitions)
- **Variations**: **GENERATE 20-25 MINIMUM**. This is the most critical shot in the entire video. Selection criteria in order of priority:
  1. Emotion transition believability (curiosity -> recognition -> tears)
  2. Facial imperfection realism (wrinkles, mole, gray roots all visible)
  3. Hand tension on phone (knuckles whitening, veins)
  4. Tear realism (single tear, not waterfall crying)
- **Watch for**: AI emotional faces often look "performed" -- like an actor doing a crying scene. Reject anything that looks rehearsed. Also reject any output where the face is too symmetrical, skin too smooth, or where the mole/gray roots are missing. The cardigan must look worn, not new.
- **Backup plan**: If no single generation nails the full transition, consider splitting into two 2.5s clips (curiosity-to-recognition + recognition-to-tears) and cutting in post.

## SHOT 5 -- Voyeuristic Medium Shot, Mother Crying (20-25 s)

### Full Prompt

iPhone 14 vertical video, 9:16, shot from a doorway approximately 3 meters away using digital zoom (2-3x), creating slight image quality degradation and compression artifacts. A medium shot of the same woman (55-60) sitting at a kitchen table, now crying softly. She holds the phone in one hand and presses the other hand to her mouth. On the table in front of her: the unwrapped Japanese glass furin wind chime, torn pieces of cream washi paper scattered around it, a few small pieces of torn paper on the floor. The kitchen behind her: a dish drying rack with 3-4 plates and a glass, a wall calendar showing the current month with handwritten notes on some dates, a half-open drawer, a fridge with 2-3 magnets and a takeaway menu stuck to it. The overhead kitchen light is fluorescent, slightly cold and unflattering. The doorframe is visible on both sides of the shot, creating a natural vignette. The camera is slightly unsteady, as if the person filming is trying not to be noticed. The woman is unaware she is being filmed. Slight autofocus hunting as the camera adjusts between the doorframe edge and the woman. NOT cinematic, NOT professional framing, NOT color graded, NOT studio. This is a voyeuristic family moment captured through a doorway by someone who doesn't want to interrupt.

### Environment Checklist (Expert 5)

- [ ] Doorframe visible on both edges of shot (natural vignette)
- [ ] Kitchen table with unwrapped furin clearly visible
- [ ] Torn washi paper scattered on table and a piece or two on floor
- [ ] Dish drying rack with plates and a glass (not empty, not overloaded)
- [ ] Wall calendar with handwritten notes on some dates
- [ ] Half-open drawer
- [ ] Fridge with magnets and a takeaway menu
- [ ] Fluorescent overhead light (cold, slightly harsh)
- [ ] Digital zoom quality degradation visible (slight softness, compression)

### UGC Test Criteria

The voyeuristic angle is the key. The viewer must feel like they are peeking through a doorway at a private moment. The digital zoom quality loss must be perceptible (slight softness, not razor-sharp). The doorframe edges must be visible. The woman must appear unaware of the camera. If a viewer feels like they are intruding on something private and intimate, the shot passes.

### Generation Notes

- **Model**: Kling 2.1 (for kitchen detail and zoom quality simulation)
- **Variations**: Generate 10-12. Select for best "digital zoom" quality feel and most convincing doorway framing.
- **Watch for**: AI tends to produce overly sharp images even when asked for zoom degradation. May need to add slight blur and compression in post if the AI output is too clean. Also watch for kitchens that look like catalog photos -- need real-life clutter and imperfection.
- **Post-production note**: Consider adding subtle autofocus hunting effect in post if the AI doesn't produce it convincingly.

## SHOT 6 -- Furin Hanging, Breeze, Prismatic Light (25-30 s)

### Full Prompt

iPhone 14 vertical video, 9:16, shot from inside a room looking out through a window to a small balcony. A Japanese glass furin (wind chime) hangs from the balcony railing or overhead bar, swaying gently in a light breeze. The furin is clear or pale blue glass, catching sunlight and casting small prismatic rainbow reflections that dance on the window frame and interior wall. The window frame is slightly dirty, with dust and a small cobweb in the upper corner. On the interior windowsill: a forgotten coffee cup (half-full, cold), a small potted plant that needs watering (slightly drooping leaves). Through the window beyond the balcony: a typical French suburban view -- neighboring apartment buildings or houses with gray/beige facades, some with shutters, a street with parked cars, maybe a distant tree line. The iPhone camera autofocus hunts between the furin in the foreground and the suburban background, creating moments where the furin goes soft and the background sharpens, then refocuses back. Late afternoon golden light. The furin's paper strip (tanzaku) flutters with Japanese characters on it. A faint wind sound. NOT cinematic drone shot, NOT perfect focus, NOT color graded, NOT studio product shot. This is someone standing in their apartment filming a beautiful object hanging on their balcony with their phone, amazed by the light it catches.

### Environment Checklist (Expert 5)

- [ ] Dirty window frame (dust, small cobweb in corner)
- [ ] Forgotten coffee cup on windowsill (half-full, clearly cold/stale)
- [ ] Small potted plant on windowsill, slightly wilted/drooping
- [ ] French suburban view through window (apartment facades, shutters, parked cars)
- [ ] Interior wall visible with prismatic light reflections dancing on it
- [ ] Furin tanzaku (paper strip) fluttering, Japanese characters visible
- [ ] Late afternoon golden light quality
- [ ] Autofocus hunting between furin (foreground) and background

### UGC Test Criteria

This is the poetic closer. The furin must look like a real handcrafted glass object, not a CGI render. The prismatic reflections must feel natural (small, dancing, not a full rainbow). The dirty window frame and forgotten coffee cup ground the poetry in reality. The French suburban view must be recognizably French (not American, not Asian). If a viewer thinks "that's beautiful but clearly filmed in a real apartment," the shot passes.

### Generation Notes

- **Model**: Sora (stronger at environmental/atmospheric shots and light physics) -- also test Kling 2.1 for comparison.
- **Variations**: Generate 10-12. Selection priority:
  1. Prismatic light realism (subtle, not over-the-top rainbow effects)
  2. Autofocus hunting believability
  3. French suburban exterior recognizability
  4. Window grime and lived-in interior details
- **Watch for**: AI tends to over-dramatize light effects (too much rainbow, too many lens flares). The reflections should be small, subtle, and natural. Also watch for windows that look too clean and backgrounds that look generic rather than specifically French suburban.
- **Post-production note**: The autofocus hunting may need to be enhanced in post. Consider a 0.5s focus pull effect applied manually if the AI output maintains too-sharp focus throughout.
